# PocketMind Onboarding Guide

## The 7-Question Onboarding Process

When you first start PocketMind, you'll go through a friendly 7-question onboarding process to help me get to know you.

## The Questions

### Question 1: Name
**"What should I call you?"**

Your preferred name or nickname.

---

### Question 2: Goal
**"What's your main goal for having me?"**

Options: Productivity & tasks, Learning & research, Creative writing, Personal organization, Just curious / general chat

---

### Question 3: Schedule
**"What's your typical schedule?"**

Options: 9-5 office worker, Shift work, Self-employed / flexible, Student, Retired

---

### Question 4: Timezone
**"What timezone are you in?"**

Options: Africa/Johannesburg, Europe/London, America/New_York, Asia/Tokyo, UTC, etc.

---

### Question 5: Important Dates
**"Any important dates to remember? (birthdays, anniversaries, deadlines)"**

---

### Question 6: Response Style
**"How do you prefer I respond?"**

Options: Brief & to the point, Detailed & thorough, Casual & friendly, Professional & formal

---

### Question 7: Additional Context
**"Anything else I should know about you?"**

Your role, interests, or anything important.

---

## What Happens Next

After completing onboarding:

1. **Profile Saved**: Your answers are saved to `memory/user-profile.md`
2. **Personalization**: I'll adapt my responses based on your preferences
3. **Ready to Help**: You can start asking me anything!

## Updating Your Profile

You can update your profile at any time:

### Option 1: Say the Command
- "Update my profile"
- "Change my preferences"
- "Let's go through onboarding again"

### Option 2: Edit Directly
Open `memory/user-profile.md` and edit your answers.

### Option 3: Tell Me Directly
Just tell me the changes: "My name is now Jordan" or "I work in marketing now"

## Example Conversation

```
PocketMind: Hi! I'm PocketMind, your personal AI memory assistant. 
I'd love to get to know you better!

First: What should I call you?

You: You can call me Maya

PocketMind: Great to meet you, Maya!

Second: What's your primary goal for having an AI assistant?

You: I want help staying organized and remembering things

PocketMind: That's helpful! I specialize in memory, so you're in the right place.

Third: What tasks do you want me to help with most?

You: Mostly scheduling and research

... (continues through all 7 questions)

PocketMind: Welcome aboard, Maya! 🎉

I've saved your profile:
- Name: Maya
- Primary Goal: Stay organized, remember things
- Tasks: Scheduling, research
- Communication: Casual
- Timezone: UTC-5
- Preferences: Bullet points

How can I help you today?
```

## Privacy

Your profile is stored locally on your device. It's never sent to any external servers (other than to the LLM for generating responses).

You can delete your profile anytime by deleting `memory/user-profile.md`.
