# VIDEO-001: Free AI Video Generation Limits

## Executive Summary
Research on free AI video generation platforms, their limits, and rate limits.

---

## RunwayML

### Free Plan
- **125 credits** (one-time, not monthly)
- 125 credits = **~25 seconds** of Gen-4 Turbo or Gen-3 Alpha Turbo
- 3 video editor projects
- 5GB asset storage
- **No Gen-4 Video** on free tier

### Paid Plans
- **Standard ($12/mo):** 625 credits/month (~25s Gen-4.5 or 52s Gen-4)
- **Pro ($28/mo):** 2,250 credits/month
- **Unlimited ($76/mo):** Unlimited generations in "Explore mode"
