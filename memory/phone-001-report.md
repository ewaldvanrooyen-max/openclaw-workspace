# PHONE-001: Phone Numbers for Social Media Accounts

## Executive Summary
Research on obtaining phone numbers for social media verification. Covers virtual numbers, SIM solutions, and verification services with "human-like" options.

---

## Options for Social Media Phone Numbers

### 1. Free Public SMS Services (⚠️ Limited)
**Best for:** Testing, one-time verifications

| Service | Cost | Countries | Notes |
|---------|------|-----------|-------|
| receive-sms.co | Free | US, UK, CA, +20 | Public numbers, shared |
| smsverif.com | Free | Multiple | Disposable |
| freevirtualsms.com | Free | US, EU | Public inbox |

**Pros:** Free, no signup
**Cons:** Numbers recycled, often blocked by major platforms (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok block VOIP/public ranges)

⚠️ **Warning:** Most social media platforms block known "virtual" and VOIP numbers. Free public services rarely work for Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Twitter/X.

---

### 2. Free/T Cheap VoIP Apps (⚠️ Often Blocked)
**Best for:** Non-critical accounts, testing

| Service | Cost | Type | Notes |
|---------|------|------|-------|
| **TextNow** | Free/$10/mo | VoIP | Free with ads; US/Canada numbers. Often blocked by social platforms. |
| **Google Voice** | Free/$10/mo | VoIP | Requires Google Workspace for business. Blocked by many platforms. |
| **TextFree** | Free | VoIP | US numbers, ad-supported |
| **Burner** | $5/mo | VoIP | Temporary numbers, privacy-focused |

**Problem:** Major social media companies maintain blocklists of known VOIP number ranges. These rarely work for real accounts.

---

### 3. Premium Virtual SMS Services (✅ Better Success)
**Best for:** Business accounts, multiple verifications

| Service | Cost | Countries | Features |
|---------|------|-----------|----------|
| **SMS-Activate** | From $0.20/verification | 200+ | Rent numbers, pay per SMS |
| **5SIM** | From $0.15/verification | 100+ | One-time use numbers |
| **VirtualSim.es** | €5-15/mo | Global | Private numbers |
| **NumTer** | €2-10/mo | 50+ | Rental options |

**How it works:**
1. Select country/service (e.g., Instagram)
2. Rent/buy number
3. Receive SMS to their dashboard
4. Enter verification code

**Pros:** Higher success rate, many countries
**Cons:** Costs add up, still may get blocked

---

### 4. Physical SIM Solutions (✅ Most Reliable)
**Best for:** Long-term accounts, high-value profiles

| Service | Cost | Details |
|---------|------|---------|
| **Google Fi** | $20/mo | Real cellular, works everywhere |
| **T-Mobile Prepaid** | $10-25/mo | US numbers, data included |
| **AT&T Prepaid** | $25+/mo | US cellular |
| **Local SIM (Travel)** | $5-15 | Buy in person - hardest to block |
| **eSIM Apps** | $5-15/mo | Airalo, Holafly, Nomad |

**Why physical SIMs work better:**
- Real cellular network = real phone number
- Not on VOIP blocklists
- Associated with real device/IMEI
- Can receive calls/SMS anywhere

## Empire Infrastructure Recommendation

### Tier 1 - High Value Accounts (Recommended):
**Physical SIM + Dedicated Device**
- Buy prepaid SIM ($10-25/mo from T-Mobile/AT&T)
- Use dedicated cheap Android phone ($50 one-time)
- Only log into target social accounts
- Why: Real cellular, never blocked, looks completely human

### Tier 2 - Multiple Accounts:
**SMS-Activate or 5SIM**
- Pay per verification ($0.15-0.50 each)
- Use different numbers for different platforms
- Rotate numbers for bulk accounts
- Why: Cost-effective for volume, better than free services

### Tier 3 - Testing/Development:
**Google Voice (if available)**
- Free with personal Google account
- Works for some platforms
- Good for internal testing
- Why: Free, but expect blocks

## Key Insights for "Human-Like" Numbers

1. **AvoidVOIP numbers** - Platforms detect these easily (no cellular connection, specific area codes)
2. **Use real device fingerprint** - Log in from consistent device/IP
3. **Warm up numbers** - Don't use fresh number for instant bulk actions
4. **Local area codes** - Numbers matching your target audience region work better
5. **Real carrier connection** - Physical SIM is gold standard
6. **Account age correlation** - New number + new account = higher risk flag

## Quick Setup Checklist

For Empire social media accounts:
- [ ] Get physical US/UK SIM card (recommended)
- [ ] Install SIM in dedicated device
- [ ] Use device consistently (don't switch devices)
- [ ] Match account location to SIM country
- [ ] Don't exceed platform rate limits
- [ ] Maintain normal human usage patterns
